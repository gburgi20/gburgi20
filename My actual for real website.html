Here is a brief description of what I am doing in school.

I am a senior psychology major at the College of Wooster. This year, my main goal is to complete my Independent Study, or I.S. My research involves analyzing certain games and recreational activities performed by adults in order to better organize groups for certain activities. This semester I am also studying topics such as digital humanities and romance literature, as well as developmental psychology. But the class I enjoy the most is badminton!

Here is a look at some of my interests outside of school.

I have spent a lot of time backpacking in places around the world. My most memorable trip was to northern Spain where I hiked a trail called El Camino de Santiago de Compastela. It was both grueling and painful, but one of the most rewarding and inspirational experiences of my life. I also love to watch and play soccer. Part of my family comes from Switzerland and many of my favorite players play for the Swiss national team. I also support clubs such as F.C. Barcelona in Spain and Stoke City in England.
